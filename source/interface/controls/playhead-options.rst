.. _playhead_options:

Playhead options
----------------

The two buttons in this section control the behaviour of the playhead.

.. figure:: images/playhead-options.png
   :alt: The playhead options

   The playhead options

- **Follow Playhead** enables the mode where Ardour automatically
  scrolls the timeline to follow the playhead and always display it
  on the screen.
- **Follow Range** enables the mode where making a range
  selection moves the playhead to the start of the range.
