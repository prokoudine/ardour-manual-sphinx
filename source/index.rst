Ardour User Manual
==================

Ardour is a professional, full-featured hard disk recorder and Digital
Audio Workstation (DAW):

-  unlimited audio tracks and buses
-  non-destructive, non-linear editing with unlimited undo
-  and anything-to-anywhere signal routing
-  support for LADSPA, LV2, VST2/VST3, and Audio Unit plugin formats.
